Welcome to Romeo.ai!

This webapp integrates with your Tinder account to give you profile tips, stat tracking, and strategies to up your game.

Authorizing your Tinder gives Romeo.ai access to your photos, matches, messages, and other data. Romeo.ai will not send messages or otherwise make alterations to your Tinder account.

Hit the submit button below to try the demo! Otherwise...

Connect Your Tinder Account

1. Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + click here to open the Tinder authentication workflow via Facebook

3. If you see a "Continue" button, click it and skip to 5. If you don't see "Continue", hit Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + C to open the developer console

4. Click the icon that looks like an iPhone in the upper left corner of the developer console ("Toggle device toolbar"), two times - not a double click. Picture here -> here. It should turn blue when selected. Refresh the page.

4. "Continue" should now appear in the Facebook popup. Click it.

5. At the top of the developer console, select the "network" tab, then select the "confirm/" entry in the table under "Name". Finally, select the "response" tab and copy the entire text string that appears underneath.
