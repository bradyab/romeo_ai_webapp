Privacy Policy

Authorizing your Tinder gives Romeo.ai access to your photos, matches, messages, and other data. Romeo.ai will not send messages or otherwise make alterations to your Tinder account, and your anonymized data will be used to improve Romeo.ai. No personally identifiable information will be associated with your individual messages.

Connect Your Tinder Account

1. Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + click here to open the Tinder authentication workflow via Facebook

3. If you see a "Continue" button, click it and skip to 5. If you don't see "Continue", hit Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + C to open the developer console

4. Make sure the iPhone icon in the upper left corner of the developer console is selected, then refresh.

4. "Continue" should now appear in the Facebook popup. Click it

5. At the top of the developer console, select the "network" tab, then select the "confirm/" entry in the table under "Name". Finally, select the "response" tab and copy the entire text string that appears underneath.
